# Independence Quest v2 App Store Readiness

Date: 2026-04-10

## Recommendation
**Not ready for broad app-store launch yet.**

## Why
- The core loop is improving, but still needs sharper first-session clarity and more explicit long-term retention hooks.
- The app has strong thematic identity, but the launch story should wait until the onboarding/re-entry path is tighter and the motivational surfaces are more obviously sticky.
- Mobile polish is good enough for internal use and close testing, but public launch would benefit from one more pass on readability, reward clarity, and launch assets.

## Store listing needs
- Short description focused on "choose a class, take one real step, get evidence"
- Screenshots for Today, Quests, Map, and Profile
- One launch blurb that explains the game loop without over-explaining the lore

## Launch path
- Treat as internal beta / test flight style product first
- Revisit launch after one more cohort of retention and onboarding polish
